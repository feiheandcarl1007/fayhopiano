About Ms. Fay

Born into a musical family, Ms. Fay began her piano journey at age 5. She holds a Master of Arts in Piano Performance from the Academy of Art University (AAU). With over a decade of professional teaching experience, she has successfully guided hundreds of students—from early childhood beginners and exam/competition candidates to adult enthusiasts.

Expertise & Specialization

Professional Teaching: Emphasis on rigorous technical training (touch, hand shape, music theory) combined with a diverse repertoire. I utilize psychological guidance to enhance a student's inner drive for music.

Special Needs Education: I am a recognized expert in the Bay Area for teaching students with autism. Using a unique auditory-perceptual teaching method, I help these students open the doors to the world of music.

Official Qualifications: Our studio holds a valid business license and I am available to sign vendor agreements with Regional Centers to assist families with tuition funding.
