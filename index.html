About Ms. Fay

Born into a musical family, Ms. Fay began her piano journey at age 5. She holds a Master of Arts in Piano Performance from the Academy of Art University (AAU). With over 10 years of professional teaching experience, she has successfully guided hundreds of students, ranging from early childhood beginners to advanced competitors and adult enthusiasts. She is a highly respected expert in special needs music education in the Bay Area.

Specialized Autism Education

Beyond traditional teaching, I currently work with 5 students with autism. Using a unique auditory-perceptual teaching method, I help them unlock their potential in the world of music.
